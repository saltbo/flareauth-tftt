## Acceptance Gate

Every implementation task that touches a page in this document must satisfy the
page-specific requirements below before review can pass:

- The page has no disabled or dead-end product controls except transient loading
  states such as saving or pagination at a boundary.
- Text that says unavailable, disabled, unsupported, no API, no storage, or
  pending support must either be removed by hiding the out-of-scope feature or
  replaced by a working persisted feature.
- The page keeps a compact console layout: left navigation rail, dense
  page header, compact controls, route-backed tabs, and no oversized cards or
  placeholder panels.
- Desktop and mobile screenshots must be regenerated after the change.
- Unit/integration coverage must remain at least 90 percent. Journey coverage
  must remain 100 percent.
